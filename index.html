FOGGS

For my first year module, fundamentals of game and graphical system development, I created an arcade game where you try to survive and rack up score by dodging meteorites. Created with C++ using Staffordshire University's in-house S2D framework (built on freeglut, OpenGL, and OpenAL).

Marble Madness

For the first half of semester one of my rapid games prototyping module, I added a jump and dash mechanic to an existing marble rolling framework. This was done after considering other mechanics as part of a prototyping process. All coded in C# within Unity.

Side Scrolling Shooter

For the second half of semester one of my rapid games prototyping module, I created a prototype for a turn based side scrolling game. Within this prototype you have to get past waves of enemies with a limited amount of turns to battle them. All coded in C# within Unity.
